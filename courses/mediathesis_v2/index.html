Welcome to the Memory Game Mash-Up experiment! Please take your time go complete all six rounds.

Each new round brings some changes to the gameplay and/or presentation.

Don't remember the cards anymore?
Skip them by pressing on them with the Right Mouse Button.

Rules
  • Press the Left or Right Mouse Button on a card
    • Left Button: Swap a card
    • Right Button: Skip two cards
  • If one card is swapped then a skip will swap it's remaining counterpart.
  • If two equal bricks are swapped:
    • Earn points (equal to the card's value)
    • Gain additional time
    • Both cards are removed
  • If you clear a row or column:
    • Gain more additional time than usual
    • All cards reset
    • The difficulty can increase
  • A higher difficulty level means:
    • You played long enough to advance
    • More bricks are added into the field
Please 'Run Test' to begin the game.
